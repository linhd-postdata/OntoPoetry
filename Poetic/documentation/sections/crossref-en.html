has patternop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: http://postdata.linhd.uned.es/ontology/postdata-poeticAnalysis#hasPattern

This property points to an instance of Redaction in which a WorkPattern, StanzaPattern or LinePattern presents analytical information about the work as a whole, the stanzas or the lines, respectively.
