The richness, variety, and different study's perspectives that scholars find in their studies is a
 highly complex challenge but this challenge is even more noticed in the poetry genre,where each poetic
 tradition has independently developed its analytical terminology and methodology.
Ontopoetry  addresses the construction of a poetry ontology to express the scholar´s knowledge and has been developed
   following NeOn methodology.
It has been structured in three modules:
    a) core,
    b) poetic analysis and
    c) transmission, covering the essential aspects in a poetry literary study.

Here, we present the design of the Core Module, its classes, relationships and proposed controlled vocabularies. Ontopoetry Core Module has reused  CIDOC-CRM and FRBRoo ontologies guaranteeing its interoperability.
